Seven Eras of India

100 Spaces. 7 Civilizations.
One Destiny.

Traverse the entire sweep of Indian history — from early human settlements to the digital age — making decisions, earning knowledge, and building a civilization worthy of 2050.

Era I
Early Humans
75,000 BCE – 3,000 BCE
The first footprints on the subcontinent. Cave paintings, stone tools, and the dawn of settlement along ancient river valleys.

Era II
Indus & Early Vedic

Gameplay

From First Roll to 2050

Simple to learn. Deep to master. A 60-minute journey through 75,000 years of history for 2 to 6 players.

🎭
Step 01
Choose Avatar
Pick Wanderer, Toolmaker, Settler, or Thinker — each with unique civilization bonuses.
🎲
Step 02
Roll & Move
Travel the spiral board from Era 1 outward toward the center — India 2050.
📜
Step 03
Draw Cards
Land on Knowledge, Challenge, Ethics, Trade, Risk, or Event spaces and draw.
⚗️
Step 04
Build Civilization
Convert resources — Grain, Gold, Stone, Iron — into Forts, Temples, Universities & Markets.
🏆
Step 05
Reach 2050
Meet the minimum scores across all tracks to qualify for the India 2050 finale.
